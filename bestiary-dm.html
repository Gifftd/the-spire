Action breakdown — kinds, abilities, archetypes
Every action body is classified by a regex pass: multiattack, melee/ranged/flex attack, save effect, spellcasting block, recharge ability, utility. For attacks the underlying ability (Str vs Dex) is inferred by matching mod + PB to the printed attack bonus; for saves by inverting DC = 8 + PB + mod. Numbers feed the upcoming template-driven editor.
